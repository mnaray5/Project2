How to Play Hangman!

The categories in this game are: Food & Drink, People, and Places!

Once you choose your category, a word, sentence, or phrase will automatically be chosen for you. On your screen there will be a series of dashes, equivalent to the number of letters in the word or phrase. Guess a letter in the clue by clicking on the appririate button beneath the dashes, and if it's in the word, it's dash will be replaced with that letter! But guess wrong, and an element of a hangman will be drawn in the gallows. You have 9 lives to guess the word, and lives will be shown on the screen. If you use up all 9 lives, sorry you loose. But guess the puzzle without using all 9 lives, and you win! Good Luck!
